Our Expertise

THE DYME COVER

Dyme Construction prides itself on illustrious projects by delivering best-in-class building, stone, and facade finishes. The Dyme Construction team is known for their all-encompassing delivery of projects, from procurement to installation, on Tier 1 developments across Australia.

With our established industry networks and long-standing supplier relationships, we create a platform to import materials globally for our client's specific and tailored project requirements.

Dyme Construction has a history of globally importing unique stones and materials directly from their quarries. Our factory and installation teams are robust, encouraged and empowered with long company tenures, established through a robust, passionate and empowering culture, establishing the foundation of Dyme Construction.
